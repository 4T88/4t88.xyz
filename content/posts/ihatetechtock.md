---
title: "why i hate tech tiktok"
date: 2025-06-12
description: "ranting about tech content creators who spread misinformation"
---

ugh, i can't stand tech tiktok or any shortform content slop anymore. it's like every other video is some dude in a hoodie trying to convince you that their favorite tech company is the best thing since sliced bread. and don't even get me started on those "tech wars" videos...

it's wild how these creators will spend hours making these super dramatic videos about why their favorite operating system is superior, but when you actually look at their content, they're just regurgitating the same tired arguments from the 2010s. like, bro, we get it - you think windows is bloated and linux is for "real" developers. can we move on?

and the worst part? they're not even trying to educate people. it's all about engagement and getting those sweet, sweet views. they'll cherry-pick benchmarks, ignore context, and straight-up lie about features just to make their favorite tech look better. like, i saw this one video where this guy was "proving" that macs are overpriced by comparing a base model macbook to a gaming laptop with twice the specs. like, no duh it's gonna be cheaper - they're completely different machines for different purposes!

but what really grinds my gears is how they're creating this toxic environment where people feel like they need to "pick a side." you can't just like what you like anymore - you have to be team this or team that. it's like they're trying to turn tech into some kind of sports rivalry, but instead of football teams, it's operating systems and phone brands.

look, i get it - making tech content is hard. you need to be entertaining, informative, and accurate all at the same time. but when you're just making clickbait videos that spread misinformation and fuel pointless arguments, you're not helping anyone. you're just making the tech community more toxic and confusing for newcomers.

maybe i'm just getting old and grumpy, but i miss the days when tech content was about actually learning something new or solving real problems. now it's all about which company's logo you can put in your thumbnail to get more views.
